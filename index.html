Built For Regulated Teams

Keep the EDI engine close to your data.

ExactEDI is positioned for healthcare infrastructure where PHI handling, reproducibility, and auditability matter. GitHub resources should make those guarantees inspectable.

  1. Parse Stream X12 into structured transactions without loading whole files into memory.
  2. Validate Emit stable diagnostics with segment, element, and control-envelope context.
  3. Explain Produce PHI-safe facts for analytics, automation, and LLM-facing workflows.
  4. Generate Rebuild compliant outbound X12 from validated facts and partner rules.
